About us

Mustard Seed Cafe began with a simple idea — that when people are given a welcoming place to gather, something good has room to grow.

Founded by Anthony Ferraro, former owner of Little Theo's, the café sits within the grounds of Trinity Christian School, Wanniassa, serving students, families, faculty and friends from across the Trinity community. It's a shared space built on kindness, connection and the belief that hospitality, when done well, can bring people closer together.

Each morning, the café comes to life with kindergarten drop-offs, running club members and their families, and parents taking a moment to pause before the day begins. By lunchtime, students from kindergarten through to Year 12 are enjoying meals ordered through FlexiSchools — food prepared with care, intended to nourish growing bodies and busy minds.

Here, artisan coffee, poke bowls and sushi sit happily alongside sausage rolls, pies and chocolate donuts — a thoughtful blend of modern café culture and old-school comfort. It's food that feels familiar, served with generosity and heart.

Open Monday to Friday from 7:30am to 2:30pm, Mustard Seed Cafe is a place to gather, to rest, and to share. A small beginning, planted with purpose — and growing alongside the community it serves.
